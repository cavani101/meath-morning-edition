Plans Published for Navan Rail Line with Four New DART Stations

★ Key Story Major infrastructure decision that will reshape Meath transport links and affect planning, commuting, and property across the county for years to come.

Irish Rail has published the emerging preferred route for the new Navan railway line, which will include four new stations with park-and-ride facilities at Kilmessan and other locations along the route. The project will connect Navan directly to Dublin's DART network, a development nearly 40 years in the making.
